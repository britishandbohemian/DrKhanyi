Mental Health

Explore straightforward answers to commonly asked questions about mental health conditions and treatments, aiming to provide you with clarity and direction on your path to well-being.

  • What are the signs of depression?

    Signs of depression can include persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, changes in appetite or weight, difficulty sleeping, and feelings of worthlessness. Treatment involves therapy, medication, or a combination of both.

  • How do I know if I have an anxiety disorder?

    Symptoms of an anxiety disorder include excessive worry, feeling agitated, restlessness, fatigue, difficulty concentrating, irritability, tense muscles, and trouble sleeping. Professional evaluation is recommended for an accurate diagnosis and treatment plan.

  • What help is available for teenagers with mental health issues?

    Adolescent psychiatry offers specialized care for teenagers facing mental health challenges. Treatments include therapy, medication if needed, and support for families, focusing on improving mental well-being and daily functioning.

  • Can trauma affect mental health?

    Yes, trauma can significantly impact mental health, leading to conditions like PTSD. Treatment involves therapy, such as cognitive-behavioral therapy or EMDR, and sometimes medication, aiming to reduce symptoms and improve quality of life.

  • What are effective treatments for OCD?

    Effective treatments for OCD include therapy, particularly cognitive-behavioral therapy, and medication like SSRIs. These treatments help manage symptoms, reduce compulsive behaviors, and improve quality of life.
